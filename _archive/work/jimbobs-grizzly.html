JIMBOB'S GRIZZLY BEARD CARE

BRANDING + LABEL DESIGN

Starting from a few apothecary-esque dropper bottles and masking tape labels, this brand had nothing but potential to grow. The concept for the full mark came to be after combining the artwork concept of each label—focused on bears as the characters—with the burliness of a finely-groomed beard. From there, each label concept was illustrated, branded with a signature rustic style, and the rest is history.
